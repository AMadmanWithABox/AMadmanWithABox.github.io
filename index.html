A Brief History of Video Games

From Beginning to End

Since the early 1970s, video games have filled living rooms with virtual worlds of fun. In the early days, some consoles were as big as a room. Today, some consoles, like the Nintendo DS, can be held in your hand! On this website, we will take you back in time to see some of the oldest consoles and computer games, then to the present day and future gaming.
